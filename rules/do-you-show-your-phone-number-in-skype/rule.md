Often the most effective method of communication for a project is to talk directly to clients on the phone. It is better to provide a local phone number to the clients to ensure peace of mind and keep their costs down.<br>
<br>
The standard procedure of dealing with Skype In calls. <br>
<ol>
    <li>If your Skype is online, it will ring your computer. </li>
    <li>If your Skype is offline, it will redirect the call to your mobile / local phone. </li>
</ol>
<p>More info at <a href="http&#58;//www.skype.com/intl/en-us/features/allfeatures/online-number/">Skype.com</a> </p>
